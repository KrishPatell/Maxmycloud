## Design Requirements

### Brand Consistency

**Color Palette (LIGHT MODE - PRIMARY DESIGN):**
- **Primary Background:** `#FFFFFF` (white) or `#FAFAFA` (off-white)
- **Card Background:** `#FFFFFF` (white) with subtle shadows
- **Text Primary:** `#0F172A` (dark navy) or `#1E293B` (slate-800)
- **Text Secondary:** `#475569` (slate-600) or `#64748B` (slate-500)
- **Text Muted:** `#94A3B8` (slate-400)
- **Borders:** `#E2E8F0` (slate-200) or `#CBD5E1` (slate-300)
- **Electric Blue:** `#3B82F6` (primary actions, CTAs)
- **Accent Purple:** `#8B5CF6` (secondary accents)
- **Gradient:** `linear-gradient(135deg, #3B82F6, #8B5CF6)` (for accents, not backgrounds)
- High contrast is essential for readability on light backgrounds

**Typography:**
- Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar) for headings
- Monospace font for data/code elements
- Clear hierarchy with appropriate font sizes

**Visual Effects:**
- Subtle effects that enhance the professional, tool-like aesthetic
- Avoid over-styling; prioritize clarity and function
